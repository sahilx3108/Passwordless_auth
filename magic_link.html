Magic Link Authentication

Magic links are emailed to the user, allowing them to click and authenticate without entering a password.

  • Pros: Very user-friendly, no password management
  • Cons: Relies on email security
  • Use Cases: Email-based login flows
  • Security Notes: Ensure link expiration and one-time usage
