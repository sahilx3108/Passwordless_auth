Biometric Authentication

Biometric systems use physical characteristics such as fingerprints, face, or voice to authenticate users.

  • Pros: Extremely secure and user-friendly
  • Cons: Privacy concerns, device dependence
  • Use Cases: Mobile app logins, banking apps
  • Security Notes: Use local device processing, avoid storing raw biometric data
